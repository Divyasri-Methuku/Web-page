About us

Environmental conservation involves the protection, preservation, and management of natural resources, ecosystems, and biodiversity. It aims to prevent habitat destruction, pollution, and other human activities that threaten the health and integrity of the environment.Sustainability refers to the ability to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It encompasses environmental, social, and economic considerations, emphasizing the importance of balancing environmental protection, social equity, and economic prosperity
