Events

Earth Day and Environmental Day Celebrations:Organize events to celebrate Earth Day, which takes place annually on April 22nd and june 5th respectively, to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote sustainability. Activities may include community clean-up events, tree planting activities, environmental fairs, and educational workshops.

River and Beach Clean-up Days:Coordinate clean-up events along rivers, lakes, beaches, and coastal areas to remove litter and debris, improve water quality, and protect marine ecosystems.

Recycling and E-Waste Collection Events:rganize recycling drives and e-waste collection events to encourage proper disposal of recyclable materials and electronic waste, diverting them from landfills and promoting recycling and resource conservation.

Environmental Film Screenings:Host film screenings of environmental documentaries and films to educate and inspire audiences about environmental issues, conservation efforts, and solutions to sustainability challenges.
